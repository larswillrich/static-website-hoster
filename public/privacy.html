2. Overview

HostMyPage ("we", "our", "the service") provides free static website hosting. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and your rights regarding your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and processing data in compliance with the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP/DSG), the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and applicable national data protection laws.

3. Data we collect

3.1 File uploads

When you upload an HTML file or ZIP archive, we store the file contents on our server to make your website accessible. We do not associate uploads with any personal identity, as no account or login is required.

3.2 Server logs

Our server automatically collects the following technical data for each request:

  • IP address (anonymized after 7 days)
  • Date and time of access
  • Requested URL
  • HTTP status code
  • Browser user agent string
  • Referrer URL

This data is processed based on our legitimate interest in ensuring the security and stability of the service (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR). Server logs are automatically deleted after 30 days.

3.3 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (provided by Google Ireland Limited, Gordon House, Barrow Street, Dublin 4, Ireland) to analyze usage of our website. Google Analytics is only activated after you give explicit consent via the cookie banner.

When activated, Google Analytics collects:

  • Pages visited and interactions
  • Approximate geographic location (country/city level)
  • Device and browser information
  • Referral source

Google Analytics uses cookies that are stored on your device. The data is transferred to and processed by Google in the USA. Google is certified under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Legal basis: Your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR). You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or cookies for this site.

3.4 Cookies and local storage

We use the following storage mechanisms:

  • cookie-consent (localStorage): Stores your cookie preference ("accepted" or "declined"). This is an essential functional storage and does not require consent.
  • Google Analytics cookies (_ga, _ga_*): Only set after you accept analytics cookies via the consent banner.

4. How we use your data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Providing and operating the hosting service
  • Ensuring security and preventing abuse
  • Understanding how our service is used (only with your consent)
  • Improving the service

5. Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

  • Google (Google Analytics): Only if you consent to analytics cookies
  • Hosting provider: Our server infrastructure provider processes data as part of hosting the service
  • Law enforcement: If required by law or to protect our legal rights

6. Data retention

  • Uploaded websites: Stored until deleted by the administrator or the service is discontinued
  • Server logs: Deleted after 30 days
  • Analytics data: Retained by Google for 14 months
